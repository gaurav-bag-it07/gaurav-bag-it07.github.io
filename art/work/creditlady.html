Credit Lady.

Type

Crafts

Year

2020

Abstract

This artwork crafted entirely using old credit cards as a canvas. The lady on the canvas surface is just a hollow representation of human tendency to never be satisfied with the possesions they already have. The linear stream of credit card pieces represents the stream of money that persists in the lady's mind.
