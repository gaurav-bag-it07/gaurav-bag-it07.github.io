Thoughts about Life.

Type

Fabric Paintings

Year

2019

Abstract

These artworks represent some of my deepest thoughts. How big is this Universe? Can we even comprehend that? What is the concept of time? Why it only moves forward? Can we go back to the past? Is time travel even possible?



Einstein quoted and I say it here - Time is but a Stubborn Illusion. I agree totally. I am the future you lost in the past. I believe continuity persists in Universe. Everything that is being put into the system leads to something else totally random and inevitable. Butterfly Effect if you may? Something that you failed to do in the past will lead to a future that you weren't expecting at all.





I remember being told in a Humanities class of Spirituality and Philosophy that Mind and Brain are separate entities. Mind has the ability to persist without any physical form. It bends, twists and moves around as its a global entity. 'My Mind rarely sleeps' simply means that even though your brain or body might doze off but mind rarely or never does. It constantly wanders around and travels curiously throught the magnitude of the Universe.







Its a wrap.
