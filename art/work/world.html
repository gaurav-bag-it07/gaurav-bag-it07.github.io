The World.

Type

Oil Medium

Year

2018

Abstract

This is one of the initial paintings which I painted with oil as medium. The world to me is a place that gives equal amounts of Good and Bad to all its children. Emotions, feelings and all the spiritual entities within a human being can be described with shades of colors. Tree with all its branches and the greenery, symbolises a human growing from its roots reaching height to get a hold of all the good and bad this world has to offer.
