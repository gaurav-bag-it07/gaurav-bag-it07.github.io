The Woman from Earth.

Type

Acryllic Medium

Year

2020

Abstract

This art piece is called - The Woman from Earth. The woman in the painting is depicted as a thinker, lost in her own thoughts. Thoughts about her life on this earth (represented by the yellow sphere) sorrounded by the sun (deep orange) and the moon (deep blue). Her face is partially completed using the leaves and the stem. This symbolises the connection with her ancient earthly roots.
