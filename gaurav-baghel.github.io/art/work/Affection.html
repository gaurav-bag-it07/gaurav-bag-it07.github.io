Affection.

Type

Paint on Wood

Year

2020

Abstract

This artwork crafted entirely on a wooden surface represents the affectionate relationship between two lovers. The man is the cold shade of blue while the woman is in the warmer shade of orange. The sun is closer to the man which signifies the radiation of affection from the direction of man towards the woman. The Closure of eyelids on the opposite corner represents the modesty of the woman. The connection of lips and eyelashes to the woman symbolises her utter beauty. The paints used were very dense and therefore the surface results into crisp texture.
