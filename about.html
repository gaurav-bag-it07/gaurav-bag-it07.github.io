My name is Gaurav Baghel and I am a multi-disciplinary researcher, developer & designer. I am constantly pondering creative ideas to bloom the quality of Artistic and Multimedia domains.

I aspire to invent intelligent tools that can revolutionize the way humans experience & learn. I want to combine my interests in human-computer interaction research, collaborative experimentation tools, and art+film with my highest dream to inculcate artistic and playful innovation in the process of living life.


Some Facts about me


I was born in Gwalior, India, and raised in Delhi, India. I've lived in India & Taiwan. I speak English & Hindi. And I am stupidly amazed with the fact that how both them connect phonetically.

My personality is INTJ. INTJs make up just 2% of the population. Anyway, my favorite Cartoon Character is Bojack Horseman because I really appreciate its 'easy to relate' story-telling beneath the comical dialogue.
